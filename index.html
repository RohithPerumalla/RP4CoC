My Mission

As an incoming 2nd year student, I hope to serve as your Representative from the College of Computing (CoC) in the Undergraduate House of Representatives. On campus, I plan to work on tackling and bringing light to issues that we students face daily, striving toward bringing more support and awareness to mental health, as well as improving campus-wide resources by working towards reliable environmentally-friendly transportation, smaller classes, and better living/learning spaces. In addition, I aim to maximize the resources that come to us students and our organizations ensuring that no genuine idea goes unfunded. Off campus, I hope to foster stronger connections between students and companies/organizations - small and large. By building more relationships, I aim to support our drive for innovation and change and to make every student’s experience the best it can be. As your representative, I’ll always keep an open mind, work towards ensuring that your ideas are heard and funded, as well as develop essential relationships to maximize the opportunities available for the students of the CoC and Georgia Tech.
